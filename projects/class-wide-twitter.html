Class-Wide Twitter

😂 Memes for classes! 

📅 Year: 2019 Spring
⚡ Contributions: Full-stack UX/UI Design, Full-stack development
💻 Tech specs: Javascript, HTML, SCSS


Do you want to share random thoughts with your classmates? Do you have questions that you want to ask your fellow students? Or post class-related memes? Many students would like to share their opinions and post fun things related to the class subjects but found no available app available. Therefore, I designed this less formal, and more anonymous platform, where students can comfortably share random thoughts.  (Not currently hosted anymore.)
